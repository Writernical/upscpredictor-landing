üìù Question Quality

How accurate are the questions?

Our AI is trained on 1,400+ UPSC Previous Year Questions (PYQs) to ensure:

  • Authentic UPSC language and framing
  • Real trap patterns (absolute words, paired options, etc.)
  • Proper difficulty level

However, always cross-verify critical facts with standard textbooks.

What is the 5+5 split?

Every query generates 10 questions with a special distribution:

  • 5 Primary questions: From the obvious subject angle
  • 5 Cross-subject questions: Same topic from different GS papers

This teaches you how UPSC asks the same news from History, Geography, Polity, Economy, Ethics angles!

What are "trap explanations"?

Every MCQ includes an explanation of how UPSC sets traps:

  • Absolute words: "always", "never", "only" are usually wrong
  • Paired options: (a) and (c) correct, (b) and (d) wrong patterns
  • Partial truths: Statements that are 90% correct but 10% wrong
  • Current vs constitutional: Mixing de facto with de jure
What are "answer frameworks" in Mains questions?

Each Mains question includes a structured approach:

  • Introduction: How to open (30-40 words)
  • Body: Key points to cover (150-180 words)
  • Conclusion: How to end (balanced, forward-looking)
  • Must Include: Cases, committees, articles to mention
  • Avoid: Common mistakes to skip
